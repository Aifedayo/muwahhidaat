about us:

Muwahhidaat Qur'an Institute (MQI) is an online distance-learning Institute founded by prestigious Muwahhidaat platform. Launched in the year 2020, Muwahhidaat Qur'an Institute is based in Nigeria and out of Nigeria with a unique offering of completely free certification courses. The Institute was established with a drean of offering intensve online cerification programs in Islamic sciences without tuition fees.
The Founder, Muwahhidaat is a purely sisters platform upon pristine Islam based in Nigeria existing for about a year now and since we live in the Tech era, with the help and Mercy of Allah we dream of establishing an institution which will possibly offer free online certificate courses in Islamic sciences completelyfree through an interactive digital environment.
